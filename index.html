Recent Projects

Project 1

Blood Bank Information Management System

As part of my final project, I developed a comprehensive Blood Bank Information Management System. The system was designed to manage blood donations, track inventory, and ensure efficient data retrieval for healthcare providers. It integrates secure user authentication, real-time blood stock tracking, and automated notifications to alert staff when blood supplies are running low. This system aims to streamline operations in blood banks, ensuring timely availability of blood for patients in need.!

Project 2

Internship at the Commercial Bank of Ethiopia

During my internship at the Commercial Bank of Ethiopia - Mendi Branch, I was involved in the installation and configuration of the bank’s network systems. I worked with a team to ensure the bank’s IT infrastructure was reliable and secure, contributing to the optimization of their internal communication systems. I also assisted in troubleshooting and resolving network issues, improving the overall operational efficiency of the bank’s services.!

Project 3

House Price Prediction Using Machine Learning

For this project, I applied machine learning techniques to predict house prices based on various factors such as location, size, and amenities. Using regression models like Linear Regression and decision trees, I built a predictive model that accurately estimates property values. The project involved data cleaning, feature engineering, and model evaluation to ensure the system’s predictions were as accurate as possible, helping potential buyers and sellers make informed decisions.!

In addition to my academic and practical work, I am Cisco certified and hold several Udacity certificates, which have provided me with in-depth knowledge in areas such as networking, cybersecurity, and software engineering. As I continue to build my career, I am focused on creating solutions that address local challenges in Ethiopia and beyond.
